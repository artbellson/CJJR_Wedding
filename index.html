A Love Rooted in Grace

In the quiet fields of Mariano Marcos State University, where seeds of learning were sown, two hearts found the gentle rhythm of love. Clark Jayson of Paoay, Ilocos Norte, and Jovie Rose of Luna, Apayao, once walked the same paths as humble students of horticulture—tending plants by day, nurturing dreams by night.

As the seasons changed, what began as friendship blossomed into something Heaven itself seemed to nurture. Through the soil of patience and the sunlight of faith, love took root—steadfast, enduring, and true.

"
And now abideth faith, hope, charity, these three; but the greatest of these is charity.
— 1 Corinthians 13:13 (KJV)

With hearts joined in the Lord's perfect timing, Clark and Jovie now open a new chapter—to grow together in faith, to harvest the joy of a shared life, and to bloom in God's everlasting love.
